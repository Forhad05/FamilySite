Preparing Financial Documents

Biggest Pain Points. And How We Can Help

Choosing the right course and university can be daunting for Bangladeshi students, especially when considering future career paths, budget constraints, and location preferences. Here's how we help:

Career Impact

We evaluate your academic and professional background to help you choose a career path that suits your future goals, considering both Bangladesh and Australia's job markets.

Budget

Studying abroad can be expensive. We work with both you and your family to create a realistic financial plan, ensuring that your chosen path aligns with your budget.

Location

Australia is vast, and choosing the right city is important. With our team based in Australia, we offer first-hand insights into different locations and how they impact your lifestyle and future opportunities.

The Entire Process

From University Selection to Visa Processing

Research your options and create a shortlist based on your preferences and goals.

Set up consultations with our experts to guide you based on your shortlist.

Prepare for, and give English proficiency tests like IELTS, PTE, TOEFL, or CAE.

Gather all necessary documents and ensure your finances are in order.

Apply to universities early to allow for a smooth processing period.

Once you receive an offer and clear GTE, secure financing for your tuition fees.

Submit your visa application after receiving the Confirmation of Enrollment (COE).

Get ready to embark on your new adventure in Australia!

Documents Checklist

Valid passport (for you and family members included in the application)

National ID cards and birth certificates

All academic documents (certificates, transcripts, recommendation letters)

Updated resume or CV

Employment documents (confirmation certificates, bank statements, salary slips, tax returns)

Savings certificates (FDR, DPS)

Sponsor's valid ID

Bank statements of the sponsor(s)

Financial proof documents (employment/business documents, rental agreements, tax assessments)
